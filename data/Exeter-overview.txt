Bottom line for a prospective student

Best for: students who value a beautiful campus, strong academic resources, supportive lecturers, active societies, and a traditional campus-university experience.

Potentially frustrating for: students on tight budgets, those expecting high weekly teaching hours, or those looking for a highly diverse and socially inclusive student culture.

Overall impression from the comments: 8.5/10. Students are generally very happy with Exeter, with the strongest praise directed at the campus, libraries, teaching staff and student experience, while the most consistent complaints concern affordability and limited contact hours.
