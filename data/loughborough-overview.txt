Bottom line for a prospective student

Best for: students who want a strong campus-based experience, excellent facilities, good support services, active extracurricular opportunities and a close-knit university community.

Potentially frustrating for: students who prioritise a vibrant city environment, extensive nightlife, or high levels of weekly contact hours.

Overall impression from the comments: 8.5–9/10. The feedback is strongly positive, with Loughborough's campus, facilities, support network and opportunities standing out as major strengths. The most consistent criticisms relate to the surrounding town, nightlife options, affordability, and relatively low contact hours on some courses.
